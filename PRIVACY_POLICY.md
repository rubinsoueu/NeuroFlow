# Privacy Policy for NeuroFlow

**Last Updated:** January 31, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to NeuroFlow ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

NeuroFlow is committed to protecting your privacy. We design our services with your privacy in mind, following privacy-first principles.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally

NeuroFlow stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Session History:** Records of your meditation/focus sessions including:
  - Date and time of sessions
  - Duration of each session
  - Selected mental state (e.g., focused, relaxed, energized)
  - Selected task type (e.g., meditation, study, sleep)
  - Feedback responses during sessions
  
- **App Preferences:** Your settings and configurations such as:
  - Volume levels
  - Favorite presets (future feature)
  - Theme preferences (future feature)

**Important:** All this data is stored using React Native AsyncStorage on your device. **We do not collect, transmit, or store this data on our servers.** The data never leaves your device.

### 2.2 Data We Do NOT Collect

We **do not** collect, store, or transmit:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers
- Usage analytics
- Biometric data
- Contact lists
- Photos or media
- Any third-party tracking data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The locally stored session data is used exclusively on your device to:

- Display your session history and progress
- Generate statistics and insights about your usage patterns
- Improve your experience by learning your preferences
- Provide personalized recommendations (future feature)

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 We Do Not Share Your Data

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers, we have no data to share with third parties.

### 4.2 Future Advertising

**Note:** Future versions of the app may include advertisements served by Google AdMob or similar services. If implemented:

- Ad providers may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization
- You will be notified of this change with an updated Privacy Policy
- You may opt out of personalized ads through your device settings

Currently, **no advertisements are present in the app.**

## 5. Permissions

### 5.1 Current Permissions

NeuroFlow currently requires the following permissions:

- **Audio Playback:** To play binaural beats and ambient sounds during your sessions
- **Storage:** To save your session history locally on your device

### 5.2 Future Permissions

Future versions may request additional permissions for optional features:

- **Camera/Microphone:** For breath detection or guided meditation features (opt-in)
- **Notifications:** To send you session reminders (opt-in)
- **Health Data Access:** To sync with Apple Health or Google Fit (opt-in)

**All additional permissions will be optional and clearly explained before requesting.**

## 6. Data Security

### 6.1 Local Storage Security

Your data is stored using React Native AsyncStorage, which is:

- Sandboxed to the NeuroFlow app
- Not accessible by other apps on your device
- Protected by your device's operating system security

### 6.2 Data Retention

Session data is retained on your device until:

- You delete the data manually through the app (future feature)
- You uninstall the app (all data is automatically deleted)
- You clear the app's cache/data through device settings

We automatically limit session history to the most recent 50 sessions to prevent excessive storage use.

## 7. Children's Privacy

NeuroFlow is appropriate for users of all ages (rated 4+ on App Store, Everyone on Google Play). We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13, as we do not collect any data at all. Parents can safely allow their children to use the app.

## 8. International Users

NeuroFlow can be used worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- No data crosses international borders
- You maintain full control of your data under your local privacy laws
- GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and other privacy regulations are fully respected

## 9. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 9.1 Right to Access
You can view all your session history within the app.

### 9.2 Right to Delete
You can delete your data by:
- Clearing app data in your device settings
- Uninstalling the app
- Using the in-app "Clear History" option (future feature)

### 9.3 Right to Portability
Future versions will include an "Export Data" feature allowing you to export your session history as a CSV or JSON file.

### 9.4 Right to Opt-Out
Since we don't collect data, there's nothing to opt out of. If we introduce optional features requiring data collection, you can choose not to use those features.

## 10. Compliance with Privacy Laws

### 10.1 GDPR (European Union)
We comply with GDPR by:
- Not collecting personal data
- Storing all data locally on your device
- Providing full transparency through this Privacy Policy

### 10.2 LGPD (Brazil)
We comply with Brazil's Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados by:
- Following data minimization principles (we store only what's necessary)
- Ensuring all data processing happens on your device
- Giving you full control over your data

### 10.3 CCPA (California)
We comply with the California Consumer Privacy Act by:
- Not selling any personal information
- Not sharing data with third parties
- Providing full transparency
